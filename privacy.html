Privacy Policy

We built Seed2Sunlight to help people — not to profit from their data. Here is exactly what we collect and why.

Last updated: March 26, 2026

🌱 The Short Version

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing. Your journal entries, mood logs, and wins stay on your device.

📱 What We Collect

Seed2Sunlight stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your daily wins journal entries
  • Mood check-in history
  • Job application tracker entries
  • XP points and streak count
  • Budget calculator inputs
  • Daily step completion status

This data never leaves your device. It is stored in your browser's local storage and is not transmitted to our servers.

💬 AI Companion Chat

When you use the AI chat feature, your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. This is necessary for the AI to work.

Please be aware:

  • Chat messages are processed by Anthropic (anthropic.com)
  • We do not store your chat history on our servers
  • Do not share sensitive personal information such as your full SSN, passwords, or financial account numbers in chat
  • Anthropic's privacy policy applies to message processing

💳 Payments

If you upgrade to Pro, payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card information.

  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe (stripe.com)
  • We receive confirmation that a payment was made, not your card details
  • Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment processing

📊 Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how the app is used in aggregate — things like which screens are visited most. This helps us improve the app.

  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • IP addresses are anonymized
  • You can opt out via Google's opt-out tools

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage (similar to cookies) to save your app data between sessions. This is what allows your streak, XP, and wins to persist when you return.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

👶 Children's Privacy

Seed2Sunlight is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

📬 Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please reach out.
