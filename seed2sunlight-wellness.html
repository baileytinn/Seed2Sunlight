🔥 Day 1

😰 vs 🧭 Which mode are you in?

What it feels like
Panic. Jumping between ideas. Everything urgent. Can't focus. You're optimizing for today at the cost of next month.
The one thing to do right now
Stop. Open your wins journal. Read what you've already built. Then do one tiny action — just one checkmark. Momentum beats panic every time.
Immediate cash options
Call 211. Check SNAP eligibility. Post on Nextdoor offering garden advice or plant planning help. These bridge the gap while your business grows.
What strategic mode looks like
You know your 3 priorities this week. You're building assets — blog posts, products, relationships — not just doing tasks. You measure in months.
The 3-question reset
1. What one action this week most likely leads to income in 30 days? 2. What am I doing that feels busy but isn't moving the needle? 3. What would I do if I knew I couldn't fail?
Your real advantage
Deep horticultural expertise. A live website. Faith-based niche with real audience. Clear written communication. Most people starting from scratch have none of these.

🌿 Confidence Reframes

When you feel dismissed
Your communication challenge disappears entirely in writing. Online, people only see what you create — and what you create is clear, thoughtful, and real.
When nothing seems to work
You built a website, Etsy products, Pinterest presence, and a real business structure after a layoff, largely alone. That's a foundation most people never build.
When you feel behind
Most successful online businesses looked like complete failures at month 3. The people who made it through weren't more talented — they just didn't quit before the compounding started.
When you doubt your expertise
You have lifelong horticultural knowledge most people will never have. People pay real money for exactly what you already know.
One question worth sitting with
If you knew it would work in 18 months, would you keep going today? If yes — keep going today.

🗺️ Your 90-Day Roadmap

Days 1–30
One sale. One blog post. Daily Pinterest pinning. One community. Goal: proof of concept and consistency habit — not income yet.
Days 31–60
3+ blog posts live. Etsy listings fully optimized with eRank keywords. Email list started. First Etsy reviews coming in.
Days 61–90
SEO signals emerging. Pinterest repins happening. Etsy algorithmic traction building. This is when it starts feeling real. Don't quit at day 45.
